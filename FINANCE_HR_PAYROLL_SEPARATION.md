# Finance & HR Payroll Separation - Implementation Complete ✅

## Overview

Successfully separated payroll approval responsibilities between **HR** and **Finance** departments to ensure proper separation of duties and prevent conflicts of interest.

---

## 🎯 Problem Solved

### Before:

- HR could approve their own payroll (conflict of interest)
- Finance users had to navigate to HR routes to approve payroll
- No clear separation of duties

### After:

- **HR**: Can generate and submit payroll, and release payroll after budget is released
- **Finance**: Has dedicated page to review and approve payroll
- Clear separation of duties and proper workflow

## 🔄 Complete Payroll Workflow

### Step 1: HR Generates Payroll

1. HR logs in
2. Goes to **HR → Employee Manager**
3. Selects either "Branch Employees" or "Department Employees" tab
4. Clicks **"Generate Payroll"** button
5. Selects time period (weekly, bi-weekly, monthly, custom)
6. Reviews estimated employee count
7. Clicks **"Generate Payroll"**
8. System redirects to **HR → Payroll** to review

### Step 2: HR Reviews & Submits

1. In **HR → Payroll**, HR sees payroll in "Draft" tab
2. Clicks **"View"** to review details
3. Can edit individual employee records if needed
4. Clicks **"Submit to Finance"** button
5. Status changes to `pending_approval`

### Step 3: Finance Approves

1. Finance logs in
2. Goes to **Finance → Financial Management → Payroll Approval**
3. Sees payroll in "Pending Approval" tab
4. Clicks **"Review"** to view details
5. Reviews all employee records and totals
6. Clicks **"Approve Payroll"** button
7. Status changes to `approved`

### Step 4: Budget Release (Automatic Check)

1. System automatically checks if balance is sufficient
2. Finance can also manually check in Budget Release page
3. Once approved by Finance, system triggers budget release
4. Deducts amount from company balance
5. Creates cash movement record
6. Status changes to `budget_released`

### Step 5: HR Releases Payroll

1. HR sees payroll in "Approved by Finance" tab
2. Clicks **"View"** to open details
3. Clicks **"Release Payroll"** button
4. System sends email notifications to all employees
5. Status changes to `paid`
6. Employees receive payslip via email

## 🔐 Role-Based Access Control

### Finance Department

✅ **Can**:

- View all payrolls in Finance → Payroll Approval
- Approve pending payrolls
- View payroll details and breakdowns

❌ **Cannot**:

- Generate payroll
- Edit payroll records
- Release payroll to employees

### HR Department

✅ **Can**:

- Generate payroll from Employee Manager
- Submit payroll to Finance
- View payroll status
- Release payroll after budget is released
- Edit draft payroll records

❌ **Cannot**:

- Approve their own payroll
- Skip Finance approval
- Release payroll before budget is released
