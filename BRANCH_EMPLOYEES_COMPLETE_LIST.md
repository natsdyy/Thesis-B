## 📝 IMPORTANT NOTES

### Email Format
✅ **Updated Format:** All branch employees now use `firstname.lastname@gmail.com`  
- Example: `elena.hernandez@gmail.com`, `diego.torres@gmail.com`
- Old format (`.brn00X@branch.com`) has been removed

### Default Passwords
⚠️ All employees use their **last name** as the default password
- Employees should change their password upon first login
- Example: Elena Hernandez → Password: `Hernandez`

### Government Benefits
Each employee has unique government benefit numbers:
- **Format:** `{BRANCH_CODE}-{ROLE_CODE}-{TYPE}-{TIMESTAMP}`
- Example: `BRN001-COO-PAG-385835`

### Contact Information
- All phone numbers follow Philippine format: `09XXXXXXXXX`
- Emergency contacts are included for all employees
- Alternate contact numbers provided for emergencies
