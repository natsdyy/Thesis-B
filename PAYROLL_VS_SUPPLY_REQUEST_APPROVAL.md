# Payroll vs Supply Request Approval - Clarification ✅

## Overview

There are **TWO SEPARATE approval workflows** in the system, each with its own interface and purpose.

---

## 🏢 Supply Request Approval Workflow

### Location

**File**: `frontend/src/views/finance/RequestApproval.vue`

### What It Does

Finance team approves **supply requests** created by SCM department.

### Flow

```
SCM Creates Request
    ↓
Finance Reviews in RequestApproval.vue
    ↓
Finance Approves/Rejects
    ↓
Budget Released (if approved)
    ↓
SCM Confirms Receipt
    ↓
Request Completed
```

### Access

- **Role**: Finance
- **Menu**: Finance → Request Approval
- **URL**: `/finance/request-approval`

### Features

- View pending supply requests
- Approve/Reject/Send Back for revision
- View request history
- Export to CSV

---

## 💰 Payroll Approval Workflow

### Location

**File**: `frontend/src/views/hr/PayrollManagement.vue`
**Component**: `frontend/src/components/payroll/PayrollDetailsModal.vue`

### What It Does

Finance team approves **payroll** generated by HR department.

### Flow

```
HR Generates Payroll
    ↓
HR Reviews in PayrollManagement.vue
    ↓
HR Submits to Finance
    ↓
Finance Reviews in PayrollManagement.vue
    ↓
Finance Approves (in PayrollDetailsModal)
    ↓
Budget Released (automatic)
    ↓
HR Releases to Employees
    ↓
Payroll Paid
```

### Access

- **Roles**: Both HR and Finance can access
- **Menu**:
  - **HR Users**: HR → Payroll
  - **Finance Users**: Finance → Financial Management → Payroll Approval
- **URL**: `/hr/payroll-management`

### Finance Actions in PayrollDetailsModal

**When Finance opens a payroll period:**

1. **View Payroll Details**
   - Period summary (dates, employee count, totals)
   - List of all employee records
   - Individual salary breakdowns

2. **Approve Payroll Button**

   ```vue
   <button
     v-if="canApprove"
     @click="approvePayroll"
     class="px-6 py-2 bg-green-600 text-white rounded-lg"
   >
     Approve Payroll
   </button>
   ```

   **Visible when:**
   - `authStore.currentRole === 'Finance'`
   - `payrollPeriod.status === 'pending_approval'`

3. **What Happens on Approval:**
   - Calls `payrollStore.approvePayroll(periodId, approverId)`
   - Backend checks finance balance
   - Creates budget release automatically
   - Updates payroll status to `'approved'` / `'budget_released'`
   - Deducts amount from finance balance

## 🔑 Key Differences

| Aspect                       | Supply Request Approval   | Payroll Approval        |
| ---------------------------- | ------------------------- | ----------------------- |

## 📍 How Finance Approves Payroll (Step-by-Step)

### Step 1: HR Generates Payroll

- HR goes to **Employee Manager** → Department/Branch tab
- Clicks **"Generate Payroll"**
- Selects period and generates
- Reviews in **Payroll Management**
- Clicks **"Submit to Finance"**
- Status changes to **"Pending Approval"**

### Step 2: Finance Accesses Payroll

1. Finance user logs in
2. Goes to **Finance → Financial Management → Payroll Approval**
   - OR directly navigates to `/hr/payroll-management`
3. Clicks on **"Pending Approval"** tab
4. Sees list of payroll periods waiting for approval

### Step 3: Finance Reviews Payroll

1. Clicks **"View"** on a payroll period
2. **PayrollDetailsModal** opens showing:
   - Period name and date range
   - Total gross amount
   - Total deductions
   - Total net amount
   - List of all employees with detailed breakdowns
   - Current status

### Step 4: Finance Approves

1. Finance reviews all employee records
2. Verifies calculations are correct
3. Checks if sufficient balance available
4. Clicks **"Approve Payroll"** button
5. Confirms approval in popup

### Step 5: System Processes Approval

**Backend automatically:**

- ✅ Checks finance balance
- ✅ Creates budget release record
- ✅ Deducts from capital
- ✅ Updates payroll status to "budget_released"
- ✅ Records approval timestamp and approver

### Step 6: HR Releases to Employees

- HR sees approved payroll
- Clicks **"Release Payroll"**
- System:
  - Creates cash movements
  - Sends emails to all employees
  - Marks as "Paid"

## 🎯 Why Two Separate Pages?

### RequestApproval.vue (Supply Requests)

- **Purpose**: Dedicated page for supply chain management
- **Focus**: Reviewing material/equipment requests
- **Workflow**: More complex (approval → budget → receipt confirmation)
- **Users**: Primarily Finance team

### PayrollManagement.vue (Payroll)

- **Purpose**: Centralized payroll management for entire company
- **Focus**: Employee compensation and payroll processing
- **Workflow**: Streamlined (approval → auto budget → release)
- **Users**: Both HR and Finance teams

---

## 💡 Why Payroll Isn't in RequestApproval.vue

1. **Different Nature**
   - Supply requests are external purchases
   - Payroll is internal employee compensation

2. **Different Workflow**
   - Supply requests need receipt confirmation
   - Payroll doesn't need receipt (internal process)

3. **Role Access**
   - RequestApproval is Finance-only
   - PayrollManagement is shared between HR and Finance

4. **User Experience**
   - Keeps supply chain and payroll separate
   - Easier for users to find what they need
   - Reduces clutter in each interface

## 🔍 Quick Reference

### "Where do I approve supply requests?"

**Answer**: Finance → Request Approval (`RequestApproval.vue`)

### "Where do I approve payroll?"

**Answer**:

- **Finance**: Finance → Financial Management → Payroll Approval, then click "View" on pending payroll, then click "Approve Payroll" in the modal
- **HR**: HR → Payroll (to submit or release)

### "Can Finance access the Payroll page?"

**Answer**: Yes! Finance users can access via **Finance → Financial Management → Payroll Approval**. This navigates to `/hr/payroll-management` but is accessible from Finance menu.

### "Why doesn't payroll show in RequestApproval.vue?"

**Answer**: Different workflow, different page. Supply requests and payroll are managed separately for better organization and user experience.
